Our Story

Born in the 80s,
Brewed for the Future.

Cafe 1986 was founded by friends born in the 1980s who shared a singular vision: to create a warm, minimalist space in Bandar Botanik where people could escape the city's noise.

We believe that coffee is more than just a beverage—it's a ritual. From our ethically sourced beans to our handcrafted comfort food, every detail is designed to spark great memories. Our atmosphere balances dark luxury with the comforting textures of wood and warm light, making it the perfect spot for solitude or conversation.

Our Mission

To serve the finest coffee and comfort food with genuine hospitality.

Our Vision

To be Klang's beloved community hub, fostering connection one cup at a time.
